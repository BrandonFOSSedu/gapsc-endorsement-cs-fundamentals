GRESA CS Endorsement Course 1

Computer Science Fundamentals

The first course in the Griffin RESA Computer Science Endorsement Program is "Computer Science Fundamentals". This course is comprised of 10 weeks of content, centered around Minecraft and Scratch.

  1. Week 1: Introduction to Computational Thinking
  2. Week 2: Introduction to Minecraft
  3. Week 3: Coding in Minecraft
  4. Week 4: Binary and Logic
  5. Week 5: Computer Organization
  6. Week 6: Midterm and Minecraft Project
  7. Week 7: Introduction to Scratch
  8. Week 8: Designing a Game in Scratch
  9. Week 9: Planning a Computer Science Classroom Activity
  10. Week 10: Final Project
